When & Where

Wedding Ceremony

Fairview Crystal Springs

Saturday, July 17th, 2027
2:00 PM - 3:30 PM

A modern golf-course in Burlingame, CA overseeing the beautiful Crystal Springs Reservoir, we will be saying our "I dos" outdoors in a beautiful meadow framed by a white gazebo with panoramic views of the water.

Address: 6650 Golf Course Dr, Burlingame, CA 94010

A designated parking lot will be established for the event.

Wedding Reception

HL Peninsula Pearl

Saturday, July 17th, 2027
6:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Located right on the scenic San Francisco Bay Peninsula, enjoy a multi-course Chinese Banquet affair with varying dishes shared family-style and engaging entertainments throughout the night. Be prepared to party!

Parking is available at the front of the venue.

Directions from Ceremony to Reception

Driving Instructions

The reception at HL Peninsula is located approximately 12 miles (15–20 minutes) from Fairview Crystal Springs.

  • Exit the golf course and head north on Golf Course Drive toward Hayne Road.
  • Merge onto I-280 South toward San Jose.
  • Take exit 33 to merge onto CA-92 East toward San Mateo / Hayward Bridge.
  • Take exit 13B to merge onto US-101 North toward San Francisco.
  • Take the Broadway exit (Exit 419B) toward Burlingame.
  • Keep right at the fork, follow signs for Bayshore Highway North, and turn right onto Old Bayshore Hwy. The restaurant will be on your right.
