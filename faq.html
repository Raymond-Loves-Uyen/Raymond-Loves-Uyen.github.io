Frequently Asked Questions

Check out our Wedding Guide for an itinerary and additional information.

What is the wedding date, time, and location?

The wedding ceremony will be on Saturday, July 17th, 2027 at Fairview Crystal Springs. The reception will be held at HL Peninsula Pearl.

What is the wedding dress code?

The wedding dress code is semi-formal, but we'd love it if you dress your very best. If you would like, you can also wear traditional Vietnamese clothing like Áo dài or Áo gấm.

Is the wedding ceremony and reception all indoors or outdoors?

The wedding ceremony will be outdoors and wedding reception will be indoors. Please prepare accordingly!

Is there a reception after the ceremony?

Yes! The venue and timing details are provided in your invitation and in the "When & Where" section of our website. We look forward to celebrating with you!

Where can I find parking?

Parking will be available at both venues. Fairview Crystal Springs will have designated parking for the event while HL Peninsula will have parking in front of the venue.

Do you have a wedding registry?

Should you wish to honor us with a gift, a monetary contribution toward our future together would be deeply appreciated. However, we would still cherish a physical one as much.

What is there to do between the Ceremony and Reception

There are a variety of atttractions that can be done in the area of Burlingame. Feel free to go sightseeing or relax until dinner service begins.

What food will be served?

The food served is "Chinese Banquet" cuisine. Various dishes will be brought out at a time and eaten family-style, and will allow you to sample whatever you like. You can check out the menu here. If you are unable to eat any of the dishes due to allergy or dietary restrictions, please let us know and we will order a separate meal for you.
